About

RENDERAI NFT is a collection of AI-generated works of art.
Max supply is 1001.

RENDERAI NFT series is divided into four parts.
Part one represents 255 text-to-image portraits (#cyberpunk, #dieselpunk, #dystopian future).
It is minted on September, 10 2022.
Part two will also appear this year.
NFT metadata is stored on-chain.
What you see is what you get. No special rarity traits. We believe in the sense of sight and imagination.

We use the Stability.ai open-sourced Stable Diffusion image generation model.
Stable Diffusion license.

We used pngquant for lossy compression of PNG images.

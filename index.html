About

RENDERAI NFT is an original collection of works of art generated by AI.
Max supply is 1001.

RENDERAI NFT series is divided into four parts.
Part one represents 255 text-to-image portraits (#cyberpunk, #dieselpunk, #dystopian future) in the style of Zdzislaw Beksinski.
It is minted on September, 10 2022.
Part two will also appear this year.

We used the Stability.ai open-sourced Stable Diffusion image generation model.
What you see is what you get. No special rarity traits. We believe in the sense of sight and imagination.

Images are stored on IPFS and Filecoin and are copyright-free for NFT owners.
We used pngquant for lossy compression of PNG images.
NFT metadata is stored on-chain.
